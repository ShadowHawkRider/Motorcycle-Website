Motorcycle Costs in Quebec

A full breakdown of ownership expenses.

Compare licensing, purchase, insurance, gear, maintenance, and storage costs before making your decision.

Complete Cost Breakdown for Quebec

Everything you need to know about motorcycle ownership expenses

Understanding the true cost of motorcycle ownership in Quebec is essential for making an informed decision. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of all expenses you should consider.

1. Licensing & Safety Course

Motorcycle Course $1200 CAD
SAAQ Licensing Exams $166.25 + tx CAD
Motorcycle Licenses $624.80+ CAD (6R)
$18.50 CAD (6A, 11 months min) $106.10 (24 months), $62.35 (every year) CAD
Initial Total Cost: $2000+ CAD
License and Course are required by law in Quebec (SAAQ)

2. The Motorcycle (Purchase)

Entry-Level New Bike (125cc) $2,000 - $4,000 CAD
Mid-Range New Bike (250cc-400cc) $4,500 - $9,000 CAD
Higher-Level Bikes (500cc+) $7,000 - $20,000+ CAD
Used Bikes (Recommended for Beginners) $2,000 - $6,000 CAD
Average Recommended: $4,000 - $8,000 CAD
Consider a used bike for your first motorcycle to minimize loss. You will fall.

3. Registration (SAAQ)/year (0-3 years experience)

Under 125cc $450+ CAD
125-400cc $800+ CAD
Above 401cc & High risk $1150.38 / $3342,78+ CAD
Vehicle Transfer Documentation $0 - $30 CAD
Average Annual Cost: $450-3400$ CAD
Costs vary depending on your bike's engine size and years of experience

4. Insurance

Third-Party Liability Only (Annual) $250 - $600 CAD
Liability + Collision (Annual) $600 - $1,200 CAD
Full Coverage (Annual) $800 - $1,500 CAD
Factors: Age, Experience, Bike, Location Variable
Average Annual Cost: $500 - $1,200 CAD
Third-party liability is mandatory in Quebec. Young/new riders typically pay higher premiums

5. Protective Gear & Apparel

Helmet (DOT/ECE Certified) $150 - $600 CAD
Jacket (Leather/Textile) $150 - $1000+ CAD
Protective Gloves $40 - $200 CAD
Reinforced Pants/Jeans $100 - $600+ CAD
Safety Boots $100 - $300+ CAD
Full used gear set Could be under $1000 CAD
Complete New Gear Set: $740 - $2,500+ CAD
Helmet and gloves are mandatory in Quebec. Invest in quality gear - it saves lives!

6. Maintenance & Repairs (Annual)

Oil Changes (2-4 per year) $60 - $150 CAD
Air Filter Replacement $30 - $80 CAD
Tire Replacement (as needed, every 2-3 years) $200 - $500+ CAD
Chain Maintenance & Lubrication $30 - $80 CAD
Brake Pads & Fluid $80 - $200 CAD
Battery Replacement (every 3-5 years) $50 - $150 CAD
Annual Inspection/Tune-Up $100 - $250 CAD
Unexpected Repairs & Parts $200 - $600 CAD
Average Annual Cost: $750 - $2,000 CAD
Regular maintenance prevents costly repairs and keeps you safe

7. Winter Storage & Security

Storage units (yours or rent) $0 - $125+ CAD/month
U-Lock or Chain Lock $50 - $200 CAD
Motorcycle Cover $40 - $120 CAD
GPS Tracker (Optional) $50 - $300 CAD
Alarm System (Optional) $30 - $150 CAD
Annual Average (if parking paid): $170 - $2,770 CAD
Many riders have free parking; secure storage costs vary greatly by location

Important Note: These are estimated costs for Quebec and may vary based on your age, riding experience, location, bike type, riding frequency, insurance provider, and maintenance habits. First-year costs will be higher due to course, gear and bike purchase. Subsequent years mainly involve insurance, maintenance, and registration. Annual costs will be highly influenced by your bike choice and years of experience. A 500cc+ sports bike will cost WAY more than a naked bike under 400cc.
