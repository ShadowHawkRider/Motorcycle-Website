Complete Cost Breakdown for Quebec

Everything you need to know about motorcycle ownership expenses

Understanding the true cost of motorcycle ownership in Quebec is essential for making an informed decision. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of all expenses you should consider.

1. Licensing & Safety Course

SAAQ Safety Course (Motorcycle) $150 - $300 CAD
SAAQ Licensing Exam (Theory & Practical) $50 - $100 CAD
Motorcycle License (5-year validity) $85 CAD (one-time)
License Renewal (every 5 years) $85 CAD
Initial Total Cost: $285 - $485 CAD
Required by law in Quebec before operating a motorcycle

2. The Motorcycle (Purchase)

Entry-Level New Bike (125cc) $3,500 - $6,000 CAD
Mid-Range New Bike (250cc-500cc) $6,000 - $12,000 CAD
Sport/Touring Bikes (650cc+) $10,000 - $20,000+ CAD
Used Bikes (Recommended for Beginners) $2,000 - $8,000 CAD
Average Recommended: $4,000 - $8,000 CAD
Consider a used bike for your first motorcycle to minimize loss if you decide it's not for you

3. Registration & Documentation

Initial Registration (SAAQ) $75 - $150 CAD
Annual Registration Renewal $75 - $150 CAD
Safety Inspection (if required) $0 - $50 CAD
Vehicle Transfer Documentation $0 - $30 CAD
Average Annual Cost: $75 - $150 CAD
Costs may vary depending on your bike's engine size and age

4. Insurance

Third-Party Liability Only (Annual) $250 - $600 CAD
Liability + Collision (Annual) $600 - $1,200 CAD
Full Coverage (Annual) $800 - $1,500 CAD
Factors: Age, Experience, Bike, Location Variable
Average Annual Cost: $500 - $1,200 CAD
Third-party liability is mandatory in Quebec. Young/new riders typically pay higher premiums

5. Protective Gear & Apparel

Helmet (DOT/ECE Certified) $150 - $600 CAD
Jacket (Leather/Textile) $150 - $500 CAD
Protective Gloves $40 - $150 CAD
Reinforced Pants/Jeans $100 - $300 CAD
Safety Boots $100 - $300 CAD
Winter Gear (Quebec Essential) $200 - $500 CAD
Complete Gear Set: $740 - $2,350 CAD
Helmet is mandatory in Quebec. Invest in quality gear - it saves lives!

6. Maintenance & Repairs (Annual)

Oil Changes (2-4 per year) $60 - $150 CAD
Air Filter Replacement $30 - $80 CAD
Tire Replacement (as needed, every 2-3 years) $200 - $500 CAD
Chain Maintenance & Lubrication $30 - $80 CAD
Brake Pads & Fluid $80 - $200 CAD
Battery Replacement (every 3-5 years) $50 - $150 CAD
Annual Inspection/Tune-Up $100 - $250 CAD
Unexpected Repairs & Parts $200 - $600 CAD
Average Annual Cost: $750 - $2,010 CAD
Regular maintenance prevents costly repairs and keeps you safe

7. Storage & Security

Secure Parking Space (Monthly) $0 - $100 CAD/month
U-Lock or Chain Lock $50 - $200 CAD
Motorcycle Cover $40 - $120 CAD
GPS Tracker (Optional) $50 - $300 CAD
Alarm System (Optional) $30 - $150 CAD
Annual Average (if parking paid): $170 - $2,770 CAD
Many riders have free parking; secure storage costs vary greatly by location
