Challenges of Motorcycle Ownership

Important considerations before taking the ride

Safety Risks

Motorcycles offer less protection than cars. Requires defensive riding skills, protective gear, and constant awareness.

  • Higher accident severity
  • No airbags or metal frame protection
  • Requires advanced riding skills

Weather Dependent

Quebec weather is challenging. Riding in rain is dangerous and riding in winter is impossible or extremely limited.

  • Limited riding season in Quebec
  • Rain makes riding treacherous
  • Requires storage for the winter season

Training & Certification Required

Quebec law requires motorcycle safety courses and licensing. Time and money investment is needed before you can legally ride.

  • Mandatory SAAQ safety course
  • Licensing exams required
  • Costs add up quickly

Maintenance & Repairs

Regular maintenance is crucial. Some repairs require specialized knowledge or professional service, which can be costly.

  • Frequent oil changes needed
  • Tire replacement costs
  • Winter Maintenance is mandatory

Limited Cargo Capacity

Unlike cars, motorcycles have minimal storage space for passengers and cargo, limiting practical utility.

  • Doesn't have a trunk
  • You have to carry a backpack
  • Can't transport large items

Physical Demands

Riding requires core strength, balance, and physical endurance. Not suitable for everyone or those with certain limitations.

  • Requires good balance and coordination
  • Core strength needed for control
  • Long rides can be tiring, sitting position can be uncomfortable
